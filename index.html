Business & Change

Organisations set a clear direction through strategic goals and objectives. However, many fail to execute on their high-level strategies. These failures are the manifestation of complex strategic frameworks that are difficult to understand and implement in practice.

Also, at the centre of the success of any strategy is people and change. If people don’t understand the change you want them to make, they will not support your strategy and will be reluctant to change.

I CAN ADD VALUE BY:

  • 1. SIMPLIFYING:

    Turning complex strategic frameworks into simple rules (aligned to your strategic objectives) that teams can understand and act on.

  • 2. EMBEDDING:

    Bringing the team along for the journey. Embedding proactive approaches that enable teams to contribute to the shaping of your strategy from the very early stages.

    Also improving the understanding of the impact of change. Similarly, enabling the continuous involvement of teams throughout execution by enabling mechanisms to provide feedback and ideas to further refine your strategy.

  • 3. MONITORING:

    Proactively coordinating oversight. In complex organisations with multiple divisions and business units, a strategy does not always translate into the intended actions.

    Using KPIs and data science, we can proactively predict which areas of the organisation are most likely to be misaligned with the intended vision and strategy of your organisation.
